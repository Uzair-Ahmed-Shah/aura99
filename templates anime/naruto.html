Synopsis

*Naruto* follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja ostracized by his village because he houses the Nine-Tailed Fox spirit. Determined to gain recognition, Naruto dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village. Alongside his teammates Sakura Haruno, Sasuke Uchiha, and mentor Kakashi Hatake, Naruto embarks on various missions, facing powerful foes and uncovering secrets about his past.
