Synopsis

*Tokyo Ghoul* follows Ken Kaneki, a college student who survives a near-fatal encounter with a ghoul, only to find himself transformed into one. Living as a half-ghoul in a world where humans and ghouls are in a constant struggle for survival, Kaneki must come to terms with his new identity and find a way to coexist with the monsters who once hunted him.

As he grapples with his transformation, Kaneki navigates a complex web of alliances, rivalries, and hidden truths about the ghoul world. The series explores the inner conflict of being human while possessing the instincts and cravings of a ghoul, making Kaneki's journey of self-discovery both painful and poignant.
