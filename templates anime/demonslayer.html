Synopsis

*Demon Slayer* follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and save his sister, Nezuko, who has been transformed into a demon. The story takes us through breathtaking battles, moving character arcs, and stunning visuals as Tanjiro and his friends confront powerful demons and uncover the mysteries behind the Demon Slayer Corps and the demonic forces.
