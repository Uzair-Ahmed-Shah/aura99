Synopsis

*Violet Evergarden* follows Violet, a young girl who was raised for war and spent her years as a soldier, emotionally detached from the world. After the war ends, she becomes an "Auto Memory Doll," a professional writer tasked with helping people express their feelings through letters. As Violet writes for others, she begins to understand her own emotions and seeks to comprehend the meaning of "love" – something her former commander, Gilbert, had said to her before his disappearance.

The show beautifully explores Violet's journey of healing, the emotional weight of the letters she writes, and the bonds she forms with others.
