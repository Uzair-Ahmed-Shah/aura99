Synopsis

After a chance meeting, Nasa Yuzaki falls head over heels for a mysterious girl named Tsukasa, who unexpectedly agrees to marry him. However, Nasa's life is full of surprises when his wife, Tsukasa, becomes more than he ever imagined, revealing a quirky and loving side to her personality. **Tonikawa: Over the Moon For You** is a sweet, romantic comedy that follows the adventures of this newly married couple as they navigate the ups and downs of life and love together.

Full of charm, lighthearted moments, and adorable interactions between the main characters, this anime captures the joy of newlywed life, filled with humor and tender moments that will make your heart melt.
