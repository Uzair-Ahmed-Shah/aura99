Synopsis

*My Little Monster* follows the unlikely relationship between Shizuku Mizutani, a cold and calculating honor student who cares little about anything but her grades, and Haru Yoshida, a troublemaker with a reputation for being a delinquent. Despite their differences, they start to develop a bond after a series of encounters.

As Haru becomes more involved in Shizuku's life, their relationship grows, and they each learn more about the other's true feelings and struggles. With Shizuku discovering what it means to care for someone and Haru overcoming his misunderstood nature, *My Little Monster* is a charming exploration of unexpected friendships and romantic connections.
