Synopsis

Baki, a martial arts anime, follows the journey of Baki Hanma, a young fighter who strives to become the strongest man in the world. As he engages in a brutal battle with powerful opponents, he seeks to surpass his father, the legendary Yujiro Hanma. The anime showcases epic fights, intense martial arts techniques, and a deep exploration of Baki's desire for strength.

Throughout his journey, Baki encounters various fighters, each with their own unique abilities and backstories, creating an intense and thrilling atmosphere that keeps viewers on edge. The series explores themes of power, family, and the human will to overcome obstacles.
