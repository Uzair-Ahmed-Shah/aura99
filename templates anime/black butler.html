Synopsis

*Black Butler* is set in Victorian England and follows Ciel Phantomhive, the 12-year-old head of the Phantomhive family, who is tasked with solving mysteries for the Queen while running a vast family business. His loyal butler, Sebastian Michaelis, is a demon bound by a contract to serve Ciel in exchange for his soul. Together, they work to uncover the mysteries surrounding Ciel's tragic past, including the circumstances that led to the death of his parents and his eventual pact with Sebastian.

As they investigate various cases, Ciel and Sebastian encounter an array of strange and sometimes dangerous individuals, from rival companies to supernatural creatures. The dark and intricate world they navigate is filled with unexpected twists, as Ciel's own soul and his bond with Sebastian are at the heart of an ancient and dangerous power struggle.
