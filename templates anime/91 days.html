Synopsis

**91 Days** is set in the time of Prohibition in the United States and follows the story of Avilio Bruno, a young man seeking revenge on the Vanetti mafia family for the death of his parents and younger brother. Using an alias, he infiltrates the family and works his way up, slowly earning the trust of those in power. His ultimate goal is to bring down the mafia family from within, but as his involvement deepens, he faces moral dilemmas and struggles with his need for vengeance versus his growing relationships with those around him.

The anime explores themes of betrayal, loyalty, and the consequences of revenge, creating a gripping tale of crime and drama. It mixes elements of mob stories with personal tragedy, making it an emotional and intense watch for fans of crime thrillers and period dramas.
