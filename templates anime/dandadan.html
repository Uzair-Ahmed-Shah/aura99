Synopsis

*DanDaDan* follows the story of a high school girl, Miku, who becomes involved in an otherworldly event after encountering an enigmatic boy named Okaru. Both characters are dragged into bizarre situations involving ghosts and aliens, forcing them to navigate these supernatural occurrences while trying to uncover the mysteries surrounding them.

As their lives are upended by the strange events, Miku and Okaru must rely on each other to survive the chaos. Along the way, they encounter bizarre creatures, strange forces, and unravel secrets that could alter their world forever. The series blends thrilling supernatural action with dark humor and heartwarming moments.
