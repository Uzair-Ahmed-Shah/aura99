Synopsis

In *86 (Eighty-Six)*, the Republic of San Magnolia wages a war against the Legion, an unmanned drone army from the neighboring Giad Empire. While the Republic claims to fight with its own autonomous drones, the truth is far darker. The "drones" are in fact people from the despised Eighty-Six sector. The story follows Lena, a commander who sympathizes with the Eighty-Six, and Shinei, the leader of the Spearhead Squadron, as they face intense battles and uncover shocking truths about their world.
