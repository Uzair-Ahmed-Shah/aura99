Synopsis

*Mashle: Magic and Muscles* is set in a magical world where people without magical abilities are exterminated to preserve society's magical purity. Mash Burnedead, born powerless, hides in the forest, dedicating his life to building immense physical strength. When his secret is discovered, Mash enters Easton Magic Academy, a prestigious school, and pretends to be a magic user to survive.

Using only his brute strength and absurdly comedic antics, Mash overcomes magical challenges and earns the admiration of friends and foes alike. This action-packed anime combines parody, gag humor, and thrilling battles, offering a hilarious twist on the shounen genre.
