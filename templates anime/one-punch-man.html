Synopsis

*One Punch Man* tells the story of Saitama, a hero so powerful he can defeat any opponent with a single punch. Despite his overwhelming strength, Saitama struggles with boredom and a lack of recognition from the Hero Association. As he battles increasingly powerful foes and encounters new allies, Saitama seeks to find meaning and excitement in his life as a hero.
