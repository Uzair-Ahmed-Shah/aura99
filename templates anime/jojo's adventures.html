Synopsis

*JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure* is a multi-generational saga that follows the adventures of the Joestar family, whose members are bestowed with unique supernatural abilities. The story begins in the late 19th century with Jonathan Joestar, who must fight his adopted brother, Dio Brando, a ruthless villain who acquires powers beyond comprehension after becoming a vampire.

As the series progresses, the Joestar legacy continues through various descendants, each confronting unimaginable threats with their Stand powers, supernatural manifestations that grant them abilities. The narrative spans across multiple arcs, each filled with intense battles, quirky characters, and intricate plots, all set in various global locations.
