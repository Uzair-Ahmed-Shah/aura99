Synopsis

*Death Note* tells the story of Light Yagami, a brilliant high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook dropped by the Shinigami, Ryuk. The Death Note grants the ability to kill anyone by writing their name while visualizing their face. Light begins to use the notebook to create a "perfect world," free of crime, under the moniker "Kira."

As his actions attract global attention, Light becomes entangled in a battle of wits with L, a genius detective determined to stop him. The anime delves into themes of morality, justice, and the corrupting influence of power, maintaining a tense cat-and-mouse dynamic between its protagonists.
