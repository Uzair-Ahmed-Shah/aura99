Synopsis

*Blue Exorcist* follows Rin Okumura, a seemingly ordinary teenager who discovers he is the son of Satan. When his foster father dies protecting him from demons, Rin enrolls at True Cross Academy to become an exorcist and fight against his father's forces. Together with his brother Yukio and his classmates, Rin faces challenging battles while uncovering secrets about his own identity and the demon world.
