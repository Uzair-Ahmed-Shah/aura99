Synopsis

**Pluto** is a futuristic mystery anime set in a world where robots and humans coexist. The plot follows the investigation of a series of robot-related murders, with the protagonist, Gesicht, a robot detective, trying to uncover the mastermind behind these crimes. The murders have begun to target some of the world’s most advanced robots, leading to a deeper conspiracy that involves the greatest minds in robotics and human rights issues. As the investigation unfolds, themes of existentialism, identity, and morality come into play as the line between human and machine becomes increasingly blurred.

As Gesicht works through complex cases and delves into the mysteries of robot existence, his own memories and understanding of his purpose are challenged. The anime’s dark tone explores the consequences of artificial intelligence and the ethical dilemmas tied to technological advancements, making it a thought-provoking and thrilling experience for fans of sci-fi and mystery genres.
