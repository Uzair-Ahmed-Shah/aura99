Synopsis

Master of Killing Time follows the journey of a young man caught in the intricate web of time and its manipulation. With his life in danger and seemingly no way out, he must confront various forces and discover his own role in the cosmic balance. The protagonist's pursuit of knowledge about time becomes central to his survival, and he must unravel the mysteries of time travel, paradoxes, and manipulation in a world where time is both an ally and a threat.

With each twist and turn, the story explores themes of fate, personal growth, and the consequences of tampering with time. The protagonist’s journey is a blend of action, suspense, and philosophical questions about the nature of existence and the irreversible flow of time.
