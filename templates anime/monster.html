Synopsis

Monster follows Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a brilliant Japanese brain surgeon working in Germany, whose life changes forever after saving the life of a young boy who was shot in the head. This boy, Johan, grows up to become a terrifying and manipulative serial killer, and the series traces Tenma's quest to stop him.

The story delves into themes of morality, fate, and the psychological trauma caused by violence. As Tenma becomes more involved in the pursuit of Johan, he questions the boundaries of good and evil, and whether one can escape their past.
