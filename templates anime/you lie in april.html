Synopsis

*Your Lie in April* follows Kousei Arima, a piano prodigy who loses the ability to hear the sound of the piano after his mother’s death. He becomes emotionally numb, avoiding music entirely until he meets a spirited violinist named Kaori Miyazono. Kaori’s passion for music and her unconventional playing style gradually pulls Kousei out of his shell, helping him rediscover his love for the piano and for life itself.



However, Kaori has her own struggles and secrets, and their relationship evolves amidst music, romance, and heartbreak. As Kousei reconnects with his past, he also begins to confront his own fears and trauma, ultimately realizing the importance of moving forward. *Your Lie in April* is a powerful story about love, loss, and the transformative power of music.
