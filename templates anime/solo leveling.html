Synopsis

*Solo Leveling* follows Jinwoo Sung, a weak and struggling E-rank hunter in a world of supernatural threats. After surviving a perilous double dungeon and awakening a unique power to "level up," Jinwoo embarks on a journey to become humanity's strongest protector. The story is a thrilling blend of action, adventure, and mystery as Jinwoo battles dangerous foes and uncovers the hidden truths behind his newfound abilities.
