Synopsis

Kyouko Hori is the popular and perfect social butterfly, whereas Izumi Miyamura is the gloomy and shy loner. But in an unexpected turn of events, their lives intertwine outside of school, revealing the hidden sides of their personalities. Hori is a homebody with a knack for taking care of her little brother, while Miyamura is a laid-back guy covered in tattoos and piercings.

"Horimiya" explores the heartwarming and sometimes humorous development of their relationship as they navigate their dual lives. From friends to something more, their bond shows how people can change when they let someone truly see who they are. This slice-of-life romance is filled with authentic emotions, relatable moments, and the joys of growing up together.
