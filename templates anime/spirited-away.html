Synopsis

*Spirited Away* follows 10-year-old Chihiro Ogino, who stumbles upon a mysterious and abandoned amusement park with her parents. As night falls, the park transforms into a surreal world of spirits and magic. Chihiro’s parents are turned into pigs, and she must navigate this strange realm to save them and find her way home.

Guided by Haku, a mysterious boy with a hidden past, Chihiro works in a bathhouse run by the powerful witch Yubaba. Along the way, she meets unique characters like the enigmatic No-Face, and learns lessons about courage, kindness, and growing up. With breathtaking visuals and heartfelt storytelling, this Academy Award-winning masterpiece captures the essence of Studio Ghibli's charm.
