Synopsis

*Suzume* follows a 17-year-old girl named Suzume who embarks on a journey to close mysterious doors that are causing disasters across Japan. The story revolves around her encounters with a boy named Souta, who transforms into a chair, and together they must fight to protect their world from an impending calamity.

As Suzume travels through Japan, she uncovers more about her own past, the consequences of opening these doors, and the profound bond she shares with Souta. The film explores themes of loss, love, and resilience, as Suzume learns to come to terms with her feelings while facing an uncertain future.
