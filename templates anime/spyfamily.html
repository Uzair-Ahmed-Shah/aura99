Synopsis

*Spy x Family* follows the story of Loid Forger, a master spy tasked with infiltrating an elite school by forming a fake family. Little does he know that his "wife," Yor, is an assassin, and his adopted daughter, Anya, is a telepath. Together, they navigate their secret lives while forming genuine bonds in the midst of their unique mission.
