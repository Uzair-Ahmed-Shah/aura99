Synopsis

In a world where a mysterious phenomenon turns all of humanity to stone statues, the brilliant high school student Senku Ishigami awakens after thousands of years in a world where civilization has collapsed. Driven by his insatiable curiosity and scientific knowledge, Senku strives to rebuild the world from scratch by advancing technology and reviving humanity.

With the help of his friends, including his loyal childhood friend Taiju Oki, Senku sets out on a journey to uncover the mysteries behind the petrification event, rebuild the foundations of science, and ultimately reignite the flames of civilization. But as he faces new challenges, including enemies who want to use his knowledge for their own gain, Senku must rely on his scientific ingenuity to navigate this post-apocalyptic world.
