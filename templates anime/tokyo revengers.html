Synopsis

*Tokyo Revengers* follows the life of Takemichi Hanagaki, a down-and-out man who finds himself transported back in time to his middle school days. Takemichi discovers that his ex-girlfriend, Hinata Tachibana, and her brother were murdered by a gang known as the Tokyo Manji Gang. Determined to save them, he becomes involved with the gang and tries to change the past to prevent their deaths.

As Takemichi works to alter the course of events, he forms close relationships with the members of the Tokyo Manji Gang, including Mikey, the gang's leader, and Draken, his right-hand man. He also encounters many challenges as he navigates the world of gang rivalries, criminal activity, and loyalty, all while trying to protect those he cares about from the tragic fate that awaits them.
