Synopsis

*I Want to Eat Your Pancreas* follows the story of a high school student who finds a diary belonging to his classmate, Sakura Yamauchi. In it, he learns that she is suffering from a terminal pancreatic disease and has only a short time to live. Despite their initial differences, they begin to bond, with the protagonist secretly caring for Sakura and keeping her condition a secret from others. As their friendship blossoms, he starts to understand the deep emotional struggles Sakura faces, and the two of them form an intimate bond, living life to the fullest despite the inevitable.



The film explores themes of love, loss, and the fleeting nature of life, showing how two people can change each other's lives profoundly in a short period of time. Through their journey, they learn the value of living in the moment and cherishing the time they have. *I Want to Eat Your Pancreas* is a heart-wrenching story of connection and the power of human relationships.
