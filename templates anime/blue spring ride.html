Synopsis

Futaba Yoshioka was once a popular high school girl with a promising future, until her first love, Kou Mabuchi, abruptly left her behind. Years later, they reunite in high school, but the person Kou has become is vastly different. Futaba has to navigate her feelings for him, while also facing the complexities of growing up, finding new love, and reconciling the past.

With beautiful animation and a story that highlights the trials of teenage relationships and self-discovery, **Blue Spring Ride** is a poignant romance anime that explores love, loss, and moving forward.
