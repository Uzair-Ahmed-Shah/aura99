Synopsis

*The Boy and the Heron* follows Mahito Maki, a young boy navigating the aftermath of his mother's death during World War II. After his father remarries and the family moves to a rural estate, Mahito encounters a mysterious grey heron. Drawn to an abandoned tower, Mahito is transported to a magical world where he uncovers secrets about his mother and the nature of life and death.

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki, this Studio Ghibli film explores themes of loss, resilience, and self-discovery. The breathtaking animation and fantastical storytelling create an immersive experience, blending reality with surrealism.
