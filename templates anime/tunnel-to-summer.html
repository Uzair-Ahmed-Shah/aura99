Synopsis

*The Tunnel to Summer, the Exit of Goodbyes* follows Kaoru Touno, a young man haunted by the death of his sister and his family's turmoil. After discovering the Urashima Tunnel, a mysterious passage that alters time, Kaoru embarks on a journey to confront his grief and uncover the mysteries of the tunnel.

In the tunnel, time flows differently: two days spent inside equal eight years in the real world. This powerful narrative delves into themes of grief, loss, and the desire to turn back time. As Kaoru's relationship with Anzu Hanashiro deepens, they both seek solace and understanding, exploring their personal struggles and connection to the past and future.
