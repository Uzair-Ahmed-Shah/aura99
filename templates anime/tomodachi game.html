Synopsis

*Tomodachi Game* follows the story of Yuichi Katagiri, a high school student whose class becomes entangled in a game of trust and betrayal. When the class is kidnapped and forced to play a mysterious game, Yuichi must navigate the complex web of human emotions and relationships in order to survive.

The game forces participants to confront their deepest secrets and personal dilemmas, testing the bonds of friendship and loyalty. As the stakes rise and alliances shift, Yuichi's intelligence and cunning are put to the ultimate test. But will he be able to trust his friends, or is everyone playing their own game?
