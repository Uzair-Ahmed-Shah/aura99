Synopsis

*Your Name* follows Mitsuha Miyamizu, a high school girl from a rural town, and Taki Tachibana, a high school boy in bustling Tokyo. One day, they mysteriously swap bodies and begin living each other's lives. Through their shared experiences, they form a profound connection, but soon uncover an extraordinary cosmic event that threatens to separate them forever.

Directed by Makoto Shinkai, this visually stunning and emotionally gripping movie explores themes of love, destiny, and the desire to bridge distances, both physical and emotional.
