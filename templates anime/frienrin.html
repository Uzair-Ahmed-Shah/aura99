Synopsis

*Frieren: Beyond Journey’s End* tells the story of Frieren, an elf mage who outlives her companions after their journey to defeat the Demon King. Decades later, she embarks on a personal quest to understand human emotions and connections while encountering new allies and reliving memories of her past. This poignant tale beautifully explores the passage of time and the bonds that transcend it.
