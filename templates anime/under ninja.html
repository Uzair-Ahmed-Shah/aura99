Synopsis

In a world where ninjas have secretly lived among society for decades, **Under Ninja** follows a young ninja who is sent on missions to carry out various tasks. The story blends humor and action as the protagonist navigates his dual life as a student and a secret operative. With the modern world evolving around him, his role in society becomes increasingly complicated. As the ninja world faces new threats and challenges, he must adapt quickly to survive.

The anime dives into themes of duty, societal change, and the conflict between tradition and modernity. Set against a backdrop of a society that is unaware of the presence of ninjas, the show uncovers the hidden, dangerous world of these covert agents and their ongoing struggles.
