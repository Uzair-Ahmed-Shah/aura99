Synopsis

As a wild youth, Shouya Ishida seeks to stave off boredom by bullying Shouko Nishimiya, a deaf transfer student. When the repercussions of his actions isolate him from his peers, Shouya grows remorseful. Now in high school, he embarks on a heartfelt journey of redemption, hoping to make amends with Shouko and find peace with his past mistakes.
