Synopsis

*Dragon Ball* follows the adventures of Goku, a young boy with extraordinary strength and a mysterious origin. Together with his friends, Goku embarks on a quest to collect all seven Dragon Balls, mystical orbs that can summon a dragon capable of granting any wish. Along the way, Goku trains in martial arts, faces powerful enemies, and discovers the true meaning of friendship and perseverance.
