Synopsis

Misaki Ayuzawa, the student council president at an all-boys school, is a no-nonsense, hardworking girl determined to make the school environment better for her fellow female students. However, Misaki has a secret: she works part-time at a maid café to support her family. Her world turns upside down when a popular boy, Usui Takumi, discovers her secret. Despite the odds, Usui becomes a constant presence in Misaki's life, offering support and eventually developing feelings for her. This romantic comedy explores their relationship, with humor, misunderstandings, and emotional growth.

Maid-Sama! offers a delightful blend of comedy, romance, and drama, with Misaki's tough exterior clashing against her softer side, making her a compelling and relatable protagonist. The chemistry between Misaki and Usui provides plenty of heartwarming moments that will keep you invested in their relationship.
