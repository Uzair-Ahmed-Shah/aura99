Synopsis

In a world where magic is everything, Asta is the only person born without any magical power. Despite this, he dreams of becoming the Wizard King, the strongest mage in the Clover Kingdom. To achieve his goal, Asta trains relentlessly to overcome his lack of magic. One day, he receives a rare five-leaf clover grimoire, which grants him anti-magic abilities. Alongside his rival Yuno, who is exceptionally talented, Asta embarks on a journey filled with fierce battles, magic, and rivalry to prove that even without magic, anyone can rise to the top.
