Synopsis

*Summer Time Rendering* follows Shinpei Ajiro as he returns to his hometown for the funeral of his childhood friend Ushio Kofune, who supposedly drowned. However, mysterious bruises around her neck suggest foul play. As strange occurrences escalate on the remote island, Shinpei discovers a supernatural phenomenon involving time loops and shadowy doppelgängers called Shadows.

With each reset, Shinpei delves deeper into the mystery, uncovering the Shadows' origin and their sinister intentions. The anime intricately blends suspense, action, and emotional depth, presenting a gripping narrative where every decision carries high stakes. It explores themes of survival, sacrifice, and the relentless pursuit of truth.
