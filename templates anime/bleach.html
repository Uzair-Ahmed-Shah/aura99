Synopsis

*Bleach* follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a high school student with the unique ability to see spirits. His life changes when he meets Rukia Kuchiki, a Soul Reaper tasked with guiding lost souls to the afterlife and defeating monstrous Hollows. When Rukia is injured, she transfers her powers to Ichigo, propelling him into the role of a substitute Soul Reaper.

As Ichigo faces increasingly powerful foes, including rogue Soul Reapers, Hollows, and Quincies, he uncovers secrets about his family and his own powers. The anime delves into themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the eternal battle between good and evil. Renowned for its action, rich lore, and iconic soundtrack, *Bleach* is a cornerstone of the shounen genre【192】【194】.
