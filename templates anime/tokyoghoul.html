Synopsis

*Tokyo Ghoul* follows Ken Kaneki, a college student who becomes a half-ghoul after a near-fatal encounter with a ghoul. Struggling with his new identity and hunger for human flesh, Kaneki must learn to live in a world where humans and ghouls are in constant conflict. As he faces numerous challenges, he discovers the dark and violent side of the ghoul society while trying to maintain his humanity.
