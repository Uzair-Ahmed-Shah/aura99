Synopsis

*Jujutsu Kaisen* follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities. After his friends unwittingly release a powerful curse, Yuji swallows a cursed object to protect them and becomes the host of Sukuna, the King of Curses. Now, he joins Jujutsu High to learn how to control his new powers and fight against other malevolent curses in order to protect the world.
