Synopsis

*Moriarty the Patriot* reimagines James Moriarty as a sympathetic antihero. Set in Victorian England, the series portrays Moriarty as a man determined to dismantle the class-based oppression that plagues society. Using his intellect, he masterminds crimes that expose the corrupt aristocracy and empower the downtrodden.

Through his journey, Moriarty confronts his beliefs about justice, society, and morality while engaging in a battle of wits with Sherlock Holmes. The anime combines elements of mystery, suspense, and psychological drama, making it a compelling watch for fans of complex storytelling.
