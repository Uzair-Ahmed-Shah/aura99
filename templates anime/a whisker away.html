Synopsis

Miyo Sasaki is a junior high school student with a secret. She is madly in love with Kento Hinode, but he doesn’t notice her at all. One day, Miyo meets a mysterious cat who offers her the ability to transform into a cat and live a life where she can be close to Kento. She buys the cat mask and begins sneaking into his life as a cat, but soon realizes that wearing the mask comes at a price. As Miyo spends more time as a cat, she struggles with her identity and the risks of losing herself forever. She must ultimately decide whether to continue living in a fantasy world or face her real emotions and the consequences of her actions.
