Synopsis

*Negative Positive Angler* tells the story of Tsunehiro Sasaki, a college dropout drowning in debt and struggling with a terminal diagnosis. His life takes an unexpected turn when he discovers a fishing community that reignites his will to live. Through fishing, Sasaki begins to heal and form bonds with an eccentric group of characters who teach him the simple joys of life.

The series combines emotional storytelling with a slice-of-life charm, offering relatable struggles and heartwarming moments. The narrative balances light humor with moments of introspection, making it a unique take on finding purpose in adversity.
