Synopsis

*Weathering With You* follows Hodaka Morishima, a high school student who moves to Tokyo during an unending period of rain. Struggling to adjust, he meets Hina Amano, a girl with the ability to clear the skies temporarily by praying. Together, they start a business providing sunshine for special occasions.

However, Hina's gift comes with a significant cost. As they grow closer, the two face difficult choices that will determine their fates and the city's weather. The film blends breathtaking animation, emotional depth, and a heartfelt exploration of human connection and sacrifice, enhanced by RADWIMPS' stirring soundtrack【160】【162】.
