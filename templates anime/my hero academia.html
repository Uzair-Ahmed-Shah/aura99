Synopsis

In a world where nearly every human is born with some form of superpower, or "quirk," Izuku Midoriya is one of the rare individuals born without one. Despite this, he dreams of becoming a hero like his idol, All Might, the greatest hero in the world. After a chance encounter with All Might, Izuku inherits the legendary hero's quirk and gains the opportunity to attend U.A. High School, where he trains to become a professional hero.

My Hero Academia follows Izuku's journey as he faces the challenges of U.A. High, where he learns the true meaning of heroism, bonds with his classmates, and strives to protect a world that desperately needs heroes. As new villains emerge, Izuku and his friends must confront their own fears and fight to protect the peace, all while discovering the depths of their own potential.
